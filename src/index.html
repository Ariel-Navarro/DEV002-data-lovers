The Harry Potter's world

Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling. Here you will find all the information you need to know about the world of Harry Potter.

Summary of the story

The story begins with the celebration of the magical world. For many years, wizards had lived in terror of the evil wizard Lord Voldemort. On the night of October 31, he killed Lily and James Potter. However, when he attempts to kill his one-year-old son, Harry, the killing curse Avada kedavra turns on himself. Voldemort's body is destroyed, but he survives: he becomes something more subtle than a ghost, in his own words, who is neither dead nor alive.
